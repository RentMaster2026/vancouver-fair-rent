Frequently asked questions about rent in Downtown Vancouver

What is the average rent for a 1-bedroom in Downtown Vancouver?

Based on CMHC data and local renter submissions, the estimated fair range for a 1-bedroom in Downtown Vancouver is $2,800 to $3,450 per month. This is 20% above the Vancouver average.

Is rent controlled in Downtown Vancouver?

Yes. BC caps annual rent increases at the provincial guideline rate (3.0% for 2025). This applies to all residential tenancies in Downtown Vancouver.
